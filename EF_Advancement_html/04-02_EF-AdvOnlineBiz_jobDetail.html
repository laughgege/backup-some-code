About the Job

System Director of Orthopedics and Rehab

A prestigious Midwest Health System that is noted for patient satisfaction has created a new position, System Director of Orthopedics and Rehab.​ The System Director will be a visionary who will establish and implement the strategic direction for Orthopedic and Rehab Service lines.​ For a talented performer there is opportunity for advancement within the organization.​ The System Director has operational responsibility for the development, marketing, and management of a profitable orthopedic and rehab service line.​

The System Director will foster a strong working relationship with the Physician and Surgeons and conceive strategies to enhance the growth of the service line and increase market share.​ This position is located in the Midwest.​

QUALIFICATIONS

  • * Three to five years orthopedic and rehab management experience
  • * Demonstrated track record of performance
  • * Bachelor’s Degree in Health Care Administration or Nursing, Master’s preferred
  • * Prefer health care system experience
