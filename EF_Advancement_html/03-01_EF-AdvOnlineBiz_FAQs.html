What are the benefits of joining EF Advancement?

As a participant in Advancement, you will:

  • Get access to great internship and career opportunities from EF’s partners
  • Gain visibility by putting your resume and profile in the EF Talent Database
  • Develop skills that help you write your resume, interview and network
  • Be supported by EF Advancement throughout your job search

How can I join EF Advancement?

  1. To sign up, talk to a Progress Manager and fill out a Candidate Profile. 请与学习进度主任联系并填写英孚教育 名企实践项目申请表。
  2. EF Advancement will send you a personalized EF resume template and a resume writing guide to help you get started. 您将收到一份属于您自己的英孚简历模板，以及一份帮助您完成简历书写的详细指导。

Do I need to pay extra money to participate?

No, this valuable new service is included in the cost of your course or Alumni Club membership!



Will EF guarantee that I get a job?

We can’t promise that you will get a particular job, but EF Advancement’s goal is to help you get access to great opportunities and develop the skills you need to be successful.
